Shipping policy

After confirming your purchase, we ship and send the product via the method you have chosen, either via our shipping official or via the Amana Express service.

Shipping methods

Amana Express

A service that guarantees delivery of consignments to the required address within a period ranging between 3 and 7 days towards the main directions.

Shipping official

Our store contracts with a group of shipping officials in a group of cities who deliver the products in a period ranging between one and three days.
